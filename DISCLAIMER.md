# Disclaimer

Two-Set Policy is an independent and evolving research project.

It does not provide legal advice, regulatory certification, policy approval, or an authoritative interpretation of any law, regulation, policy, standard, government document, or institutional requirement.

The concepts, terminology, classifications, schemas, technical artefacts, diagrams, and case analyses in this repository are provisional and require independent review.

The project does not claim that all policy provisions should be machine-executable.

Some provisions may require:

- contextual human judgement
- formal legal interpretation
- institutional authorisation
- public deliberation
- regulatory approval
- explicit prohibition of automation

Any operational policy generated through this framework must remain:

- traceable to its source policy
- subordinate to authorised policy intent
- reviewable by accountable humans
- open to challenge and correction
- explicit about unresolved questions
- subject to change control

Case studies in this repository are exploratory research exercises and are not official interpretations of the source policies.

No artefact in this repository should be used for production enforcement, automated decision-making, or compliance certification without appropriate legal, technical, institutional, and domain review.
